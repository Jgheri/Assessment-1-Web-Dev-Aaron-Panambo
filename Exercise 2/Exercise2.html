Plot

The Perks of Being a Wallflower follows Charlie, a socially awkward, traumatized15-year-old in the early 1990s,
as he navigates freshman year, friendship, and first love after his best friend's suicide. Taken under the wings
of seniors Sam and Patrick, Charlie experiences life, repressed childhood abuse memories resurface,
leading to a mental breakdown, hospitalization, and eventually, healing and acceptance.
